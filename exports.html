Scope 2 (Electricity)

Export Scope 2 electricity records for reporting and audits.

Calculated using location-based Scope 2 electricity factors (tCO₂e); market-based uses RECs/PPAs where provided. See Methodology.

What’s included

  • Company name, period (YYYY-MM), and country/region
  • Electricity usage (kWh) with location-based and market-based emissions (tCO₂e)
  • Emission factor value, year, and source
  • Disclosure line for location-based Scope 2 (GHG Protocol)

Scope 1 (Stationary combustion — Natural gas)

Export Scope 1 natural gas entries saved to your account.

Scope 1 emissions are estimates based on user-provided fuel data. Results may be partial and do not represent full Scope 1 coverage.

Methodology: See Scope 1 methodology.

What’s included

  • Scope, category, and fuel type (stationary combustion — natural gas)
  • Period (YYYY-MM), fuel quantity, and unit
  • Emissions (tCO₂e) with factor value, year, and source
  • Disclosure line for Scope 1 estimates

Scope 3 (Actuals + Spend-based)

Vendor-reported actuals plus spend-based screening for gaps.

Upgrade to CarbonWise Complete to export Scope 3 data.

Scope 3 includes vendor-reported actuals and spend-based EIO screening; screening results are high-uncertainty.

Methodology: See Scope 3 methodology.

What’s included

  • Period (YYYY-MM), method (spend-based or actuals), category, and emissions source
  • Spend amount, currency, and EIO sector reference (for spend-based records)
  • Emission factor value, year, and source (for spend-based records)
  • Emissions (tCO2e) and screening-level disclosure
