Methodology & Disclosures

How CarbonWise calculates Scope 2 electricity emissions and what to expect from the results.

1. Scope Covered

CarbonWise currently supports Scope 2 electricity emissions using the location-based method. Scope 1 reporting is not supported today. Market-based Scope 2 or Scope 1 may be added in the future.

2. Calculation Method

Calculations use the information you provide: electricity used in kilowatt-hours and your country or region. Emissions are estimated by multiplying your electricity usage by the grid emission factor for that location.

3. Emission Factors

Emission factors represent average grid emissions for a country or region. They are sourced from publicly available datasets and can differ by geography. CarbonWise uses these factors to translate your electricity use into estimated emissions.

4. Emission Factor Year & Timing

Grid emission factors are published after the year they describe. CarbonWise may use the most recently available factor even if it does not match the activity year. This timing gap can create a mismatch between the billing period and the factor year.

5. Location-Based vs Market-Based Scope 2

Location-based Scope 2 reflects the average emissions from the grid where electricity is used. CarbonWise reports location-based emissions today. Market-based instruments such as RECs or PPAs are not applied unless explicit support is added in the product.

6. Precision, Rounding, and Comparability

Calculations retain reasonable precision for finance and operations teams. Minor differences can occur compared to other tools because of factor selection or rounding. These differences are normal when methods or factor sources vary.

7. Intended Use & Limitations

CarbonWise is designed for internal reporting, planning, and preparation. It does not replace third-party verification or formal assurance processes you may need for audits or filings.

8. Updates & Changes

Methodology details and emission factors may be updated as new data becomes available. Significant changes will be documented so you can understand what changed and why.
